About the Bootcamp

AI Invasion for Kids is a visionary 6-week program crafted to introduce children and teenagers aged 10–18 to the power and potential of Artificial Intelligence. Through engaging, hands-on experiences, participants will discover how AI influences the world around them—from the tools they use to the careers they may one day pursue.

Our Mission

To democratize access to AI education and empower the youth with the knowledge, skills, and mindset to thrive in a digital future.

Why This Matters

As millennial parents, you understand that the world is changing fast. Traditional education alone may not fully prepare our kids for the opportunities and challenges ahead. This bootcamp is designed to fill that gap by providing early exposure to high-impact technologies in a fun, safe, and age-appropriate way.

We’re not just teaching tech—we’re shaping creative, critical thinkers who are confident using tools that shape tomorrow’s world.
